About me

I am currently a post-doctoral researcher at Delft University of Technology. One of my main responsibilities is the development of an open-source toolbox for correct-by-design controllers, as well as the supervision of several master thesis projects. In these projects, we combine computational intelligence techniques with formal verification in order to automatically design controllers that mathematically guarantee their specification.
