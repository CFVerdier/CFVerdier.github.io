About me

I am currently a post-doctoral researcher at the Delft Center of Systems and Control, Delft University of Technology, the Netherlands, where he develops open-source tools for correct-by-design controller synthesis. In these tools, I combine computational intelligence techniques with formal verification in order to automatically design controllers that mathematically guarantee their specification. From the same university, I obtained my BSc degree in mechanical engineer (cum laude), MSc in Systems and Control (cum laude), and PhD in 2013, 2015, and 2020, respectively. My research interests include nonlinear control, robotics, aerospace, formal verification, and computational intelligence.
